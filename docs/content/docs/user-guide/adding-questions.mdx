# Adding Questions

## Overview

Questions are the heart of any quiz. Learn how to add, edit, and organize questions within your quiz to create an engaging experience for your players.

## How to Add Questions

1.  **Open Quiz Editor**: From your library, click the "Edit" button on any quiz.
2.  **Add Question**: Click the "Add Question" button at the bottom of the question list.
3.  **Enter Question Text**: Type your question in the main text area.
4.  **Set Time Limit**: Choose how many seconds players have to answer (e.g., 20s, 30s).
5.  **Configure Options**:
    -   Add at least two answer options.
    -   Mark the correct option(s) by clicking the checkmark next to them.
6.  **Add Media**: (Optional) Upload an image or provide a media URL to accompany the question.

## Key Components

-   **Order**: Drag and drop questions to rearrange their sequence.
-   **Media URL**: Supports images to provide visual context or clues.
-   **Corrective Feedback**: Ensures players know the right answer after they submit their choice.
